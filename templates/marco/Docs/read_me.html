Why Use It

Tip: Highly customize your templates without knowing how to input 1 single line of code has been made easy for you with the new Email Builder.

There's no reason why Not to use it. It's as simple as that. It offers you the possibility of changing text, images, adding links, moving the layout around and everything you basically need to highly customize your template without having to know anything about coding.

Customizability

Tip: Do everythign yourself, without having to pay a buck for it.

There's no catch!

The trick with our Email Builder is that only your imagination can limit your possibilities really, as you can do pretty much everything you want to with the layout as long as you fully understand it.

Change the images

Tip: Easily change the images by left clicking on it.

IMPORTANT:

In order to change the source of the image simply left click on it and put some other link in the first field. Remember, it needs to be a live online link, but this can be anything from Twitter, Facebook, Dribble, etc. Then modify the width and height, put a link on it and you're good to go!

Change the text

Tip: Right click the text to edit it just like you would do in any Word document with ease.

The new Email builder lets you edit the text just like you would in any Word editor, changing fonts, colors, font sizes, applying hyperlinks to the text and so on.

Responsive

All our stuff is coded responsively, so you can rest assured that the mobile look will be perfect. In order to test it, just hit Preview button from the builder once you've built your email and then resize the browser window by dragging the right-hand side of it until the layout shifts.
